Beijing

The city of Beijing is an ancient city with a long history and culture that has lasted for more than 3,000 years. As time passes, Beijing's history and culture are being passed on, which is most directly seen in the hutongs of Beijing. They are one of the brightest sights in the city of Beijing.
History and culture are the inner embodiment of the city, and a city with a deep cultural heritage is more charming. Beijing's has been focusing on improving people's livelihood in the process of development, and also in preserving history and culture.

Hangzhou

Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang Province, is known as a famous county in southeast China. It is the birthplace of Chinese civilization, one of the seven ancient capitals of China, the central city of the Yangtze River Delta, and the extension intersection of the "Silk Road Economic Belt" and the "21st Century Maritime Silk Road".
Marco Polo, the 13th century Italian traveler, called Hangzhou "the most beautiful and luxurious city in the world"

Meaningful Qiantang county records the beauty of Hangzhou through the ages.  As a world-class city, Hangzhou had a population ten times that of Venice, the most prosperous city in the West, in the Southern Song Dynasty.
 After the Grand Canal was dug, diverse cultures collided and became "the first state in southeast China", showing the openness and compatibility of canal culture.  The prosperous and exquisite Hangzhou mansion is no less than that of London at the same time.
 The multi-trade exchanges, the local dialect is mixed, the flavor of the fusion of north and south, from the early southern Song dynasty to the present.
Hangzhou cuisine, the core of Zhejiang food culture, is an important school of Zhejiang cuisine among the eight traditional Chinese cuisines. Originated from the land of literati, the dishes are elegant, delicate and exquisite. The richness of the produce in the land of fish and rice, and the beauty of the cuisine, have a reputation.
Hangzhou cuisine is mainly salty and slightly sweet, with the characteristics of "light". The specialties include West Lake fish with vinegar, Dongpo meat, Longjing shrimp, dried bamboo shoots and old duck in pot, and eight treasures of tofu.
Traditional Crafts
The rich and colorful specialties such as West Lake Longjing tea, Wang Xingji fans, Zhang Xiaoquan scissors, etc. exude a rich Hangzhou flavor, so why not immerse yourself in the watery village of Jiangnan and see the handicraft work handed down by Qiantang craftsmen for a hundred years.

Sanya

In addition to the beautiful tropical tourist scenery, Hainan has a lot of unique culture! Hainan Sanya has many national and provincial intangible cultural heritage: Li traditional spinning and weaving techniques, Li firewood dance, wood drilling and fire retrieval techniques, Yazhou fol songs, Miao Panhuang dance, conch girls, Li folk stories, boat-dwelling songs, Li primitive pottery making techniques, etc.
The technique of drilling wood for fire is one of the national intangible cultural heritages, and is one of the local traditional handicraft techniques in Hainan Province.
