Nanshan Cultural Tourism Zone

Nanshan Cultural Tourism Zone is a famous religious and longevity cultural scenic spot in China, where you can enjoy the natural beauty of the mountains and the sea, as well as visit many famous Buddhist sites and the world-famous "Nanshan Sea Goddess of Mercy" to get the spiritual cleansing of Buddhist culture and experience the pleasure of returning to the nature.
Yanoda Rainforest Cultural Area

Yanoda Rainforest Cultural Area is mainly composed of Rainforest Valley, Dream Valley and Three Dao Valley scenic spots, where you can see the original tropical rainforest landscape. Rainforest Valley and Dream Valley to Li, Miao culture as the theme, combined with tropical rainforest, canyons and other natural landscapes, enjoy the original scenery and folk customs.

Dadonghai

Dadonghai is a bay closer to the center of Sanya, traffic, dining and accommodation are more convenient.
Dadonghai is developed early, facilities are also complete, there are a variety of water activities and beach sports all year round, diving mostly snorkeling, no basic swimming do not have to worry about, because the lift is controlled by the coach, all you have to do is just a pleasant swim and enjoy.
